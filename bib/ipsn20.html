Example of citing this paper:
\cite{ipsn20-localization} introduces a multiple transmitter localization method, namely MAP_{*}, that uses a hypotheses-based Bayesian approach in conjunction with a divide-and-conqure strategy to first localize "isolated" intruders and then localize the remaining intruders.
\cite{ipsn20-localization} also introduces a novel spectrum interpolation method, namely ILDW, which interpolates an unknown RSSI at a location with the surrounding known RSSI values in the logarithm distance domain. Through simulations using data generated from SPLAT!, ILDW's interpolation error is significanly lower than IDW.
